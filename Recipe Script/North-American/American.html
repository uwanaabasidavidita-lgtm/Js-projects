Gumbo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roux
  • 1 lb chicken or sausage
  • 1 cup okra
  • 1 cup bell peppers
  • 1 cup onions
  • 1 cup celery
  • 2 tbsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the roux over medium heat until it turns a dark brown color, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
  2. Add the chicken or sausage to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the okra, bell peppers, onions, and celery to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in the cayenne pepper, thyme, and bay leaves. Add enough water or broth to cover the ingredients and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the gumbo simmer for at least 1 hour, allowing the flavors to meld together. Stir occasionally and add more water or broth if needed.
  6. Remove the bay leaves before serving. Serve the gumbo hot over rice or with crusty bread on the side.

Lobster Roll

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb cooked lobster meat, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hot dog buns
  • Butter for toasting the buns

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped lobster meat, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and chopped chives. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium heat. Butter the hot dog buns and toast them in the skillet until they are golden brown on both sides.
  3. Fill each toasted bun with a generous amount of the lobster mixture.
  4. Serve the lobster rolls immediately, garnished with additional chives if desired. Enjoy!

Brownie Sundae

Ingredients:

  • 1 batch of your favorite brownies, cut into squares
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped nuts (optional)
  • Maraschino cherries (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place a brownie square in a serving bowl or on a plate.
  2. Top the brownie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup over the ice cream and brownie.
  4. Add a dollop of whipped cream on top.
  5. Sprinkle chopped nuts over the whipped cream if desired.
  6. Finish with a maraschino cherry on top if you like.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious brownie sundae!
